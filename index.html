Hi!

I'm currently a fourth-year undergraduate student majoring in Software Engineering at the Guangdong University of Foreign Studies. Upon completing my studies here, I'm expected to graduate at 2025, with a Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering. Also, I finished my year-3 study as a visiting student at UC Berkeley.

I am generally interested in the Safety, explainability, generalization, applications of modern LLMs.

During my bachelor studies, I dedicated as a research assistant in the DM Lab (Data Mining Lab) at GDUFS, and have done researches on LM powered data augmentation, low-resource language representation, distantly supervised relation extraction and sentiment analysis for Media Contents, advised by Prof. Lianxi Wang.

Prior to my PhD, I am a research intern student at UMass Boston, studying Jailbreak problems of LLMs with Prof. Shichao Pei.
