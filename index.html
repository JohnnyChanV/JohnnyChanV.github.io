My name is Zhuowei/Johnny. I'm a CS PhD student at University of Pittsburgh, closely work with Prof. Lorraine Li.

I completed my bachelor study at Guangdong University of Foreign Studies - 广外 and UC Berkeley (Year-3), with a degree in Software Engineering. During the undergrad study, I actively work with Lianxi Wang from Data Mining Lab at GDUFS, and Shichao Pei from UMass Boston.
