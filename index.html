Hi!

I'm currently a fourth-year undergraduate student majoring in Software Engineering at the Guangdong University of Foreign Studies. Upon completing my studies here, I'm expected to graduate at 2025, with a Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering. Also, I just completed my program in UC Berkeley as a visiting student.

My interest lies in the area of Safe & Trustworthy LLM, Data Mining, and Low-resource NLP.

For the recent two years, I dedicated as a research assistant in the DM Lab (Data Mining Lab) at GDUFS, and have done researches on LM powered data augmentation, low-resource language representation, distantly supervised relation extraction and sentiment analysis for Media Contents, advised by Prof. Lianxi Wang.

Currently, I am a research intern student at UMass Boston, studying Jailbreak problems of LLM supervised by Dr. Shichao Pei.
